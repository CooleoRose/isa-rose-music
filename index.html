"Music. It's in my genes."

I am a 15 year old musician that has been actively learning music since the age of 6, in 2011, when I first took up the violin.

Currently living in Melbourne, I attend Camberwell Grammar School, where I create music, produce music, study music and regularly partake in various Musical groups, which include:

  • Senior School Orchestra, Violin 1
  • Senior School Chamber Orchestra, Violin 1 and Viola
  • Highton Strings, Violin 1 and Viola

Past Concerts and Achievements

  1. Boroondara Eisteddfod - 2015 and 2016 - Balwyn Primary School
  2. Oxfam - 2015 and 2016 - Balwyn Primary School
  3. Camberwell Grammar Student Concerts - 2017 to present (including one at Melbournes Hamer Hall)
